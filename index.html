This report sums up the analysis of my website's performance from April 1 to April 30, 2017.

The key method which I used to drive traffic to my website is through promotion on Facebook and putting it on Stumbleupon to gain more exposure.

After promotion on Facebook, there's a lack of traffic after the peak. So I decided to create another link and put it on Facebook for driving traffic.

After using different methods, the most effective is still promotion on Facebook where my friends and family actively click and explore my website. But for future investment, more time should be put on content creation and learning about SEO.
